## Status

New 5.10

## Syntax

`PREHP:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Number (minimum Hitpoints)



What it does
------------

Makes the amount of hitpoints a character has a prerequisite.

Examples
--------

`PREHP:50`

The character must have at least 50 hp.
