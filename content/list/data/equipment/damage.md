## Status

None

## Syntax

`DAMAGE:x`

## Parameters

-   x: Text (Item Damage)



What it does
------------

The damage that a weapon does, this is part of the character sheet
output.

Example
-------

`DAMAGE:1d6`

Item damage is 1d6.
